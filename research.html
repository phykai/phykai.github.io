Research

Non-Hermitian Physics

We study non-Hermitian band topology, skin effects, exceptional points, and open-boundary spectral structures.

Open Quantum Systems

We investigate many-body dynamics, Liouvillian evolution, mixed-state phases, and dynamical quantum phase transitions.

Tensor-Network and Numerical Methods

We use analytical and numerical methods, including exact diagonalization, tensor networks, and effective field-theory approaches.
